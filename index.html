What are you doing for your planet?

A huge part of the entire planet is being destroyed by us, human beings. Politicians and leaders of powerful countries get together frequently to discuss what can be done to prevent global warming. But what are YOU doing, as a habitant of this planet? FIGHT 4 GREEN is a non-profitable organization that aims to bring people together in an attempt to join power and fight the destruction of our natural resources and stop - or at least minimize - climate changing and global warming.
